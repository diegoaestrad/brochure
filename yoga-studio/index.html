About Us

Lotus Flow Yoga opened as a neighborhood studio where judgment stays at the door and curiosity is welcome.

Teachers emphasize safe alignment, accessible modifications, and the mental benefits of consistent practice.

Whether you seek strength, stress relief, or community, there is a class for you here.
