About Us

Paws & Claws Grooming began as a small salon focused on patience and positive experiences for nervous pets.

Groomers continue education on breed standards, skin conditions, and low-stress handling techniques.

We treat every pet like family and send them home comfortable, clean, and tail-wag ready.
